Interior Digitization

Doll house

As part of our interior scanning package, we provide a doll house view of the scanned interior spaces. This is an external, all-perspective view of the space that can be rotated and viewed from multiple angles.

Floor plan

A floor plan can be extracted from the scan, as an add-on to the interior scanning package. This provides clients with a footprint and layout of the space with measurements. A floor plan can show clients, such as home buyers, the number and location of bedrooms and bathrooms in a property, as well as the size of each room.

Our Services

Tuple specializes in creating ‘digital twins’ - capturing and creating immersive and precise virtual representations of interior spaces, through the application of structured light (infrared) 3D sensor technology. We create and host virtual tours, based on our clients’ needs – whether for real estate, virtual events, architectural planning or more. Our accurate modeling enables the viewer virtually explore, and even measure interior spaces. As part of our interior scanning package, we also include a 3D model “doll house” view that our clients can use to quickly rotate and view different parts of the model as part of the tour.

As an add-on to our interior scanning package, we can provide floor plans with measurement as well as photography services. More information can be found on our rates can be found on our Rates Page.

The Benefits of Interior Digitization

The digitization of interior spaces can be applied across multiple industries, and provides a host of benefits by better engaging customers, enabling accurate planning, and creating accurate documentation.

Examples of industries leveraging digitization solutions include:

  • Real Estate – cast a wider net, by allowing prospective home-buyers to view listings virtually. Find out how using virtual attracts customers by 82%. Find out how using virtual attracts customers by 82%.
  • Hospitality – increase engagement by providing virtual tours of accommodation and rental spaces.
  • Architecture – use virtual tours to plan, collaborate and design new constructions with your team.
  • Insurance – quickly capture visual data used in property disputes and claims.
  • Museums and Galleries – use the virtual tour to provide 24/7 access for visitors or event planners and for hosting virtual events.
