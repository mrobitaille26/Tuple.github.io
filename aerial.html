Our Services

Using a DJI Phantom 4 RTK drone, piloted by a Transport Canada Advance Operations certified drone operator, Tuple can capture exterior objects in unrestricted, as well as in several restricted airspaces.

The DJI Phantom 4 RTK drone has an integrated photogrammetry application that enables automated survey of an area. Through this technology Tuple can extract 3D information from sequenced photographs. This technology can be used to create digital 3D models of exterior objects, such as houses, buildings, external art structures, or natural formations. This data can be also be used to develop 3D printed models.
